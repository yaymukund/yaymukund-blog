**Sleeping Alone**

<pre class="poem">
Listening
to the neighbor's dog
bark
and bark.
He must
be very big,
his mouth as big
as my head,
his head
as big
as a Macy's Day float,
his corridor throat
commanding me
to hear:
I am one
dog
in the lonely bowl
of night
and there
is so
much
dark.
</pre>

-- Amy Miller, 2018
